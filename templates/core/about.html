About ScrapeWale

Revolutionizing waste management through technology and transparency.

ScrapeWale is a digital platform designed to bridge the gap between households and scrap recyclers. We believe in a cleaner, greener planet where waste disposal is convenient, fair, and eco-friendly.

Our mission is to organize the unorganized waste sector, providing fair rates to sellers and dignified livelihoods to pickup agents.
